Lemon Quality Detection

Our project uses machine learning and image processing to automatically detect the quality of lemons. By analyzing visual features like texture, color, and defects, it classifies lemons as fresh, damaged, or rotten. This smart solution reduces manual effort, improves accuracy, and speeds up the sorting process. Designed for use in agriculture and supply chains, it helps farmers and vendors make better decisions. The system is a step toward modern, tech-driven food quality control..
